# Initial Concept

Australian rules score tracking app designed for goal umpires, team managers, and coaches.

## Product Definition

## Target Users

- **Goal Umpires:** Official score tracking during matches, requiring high accuracy and a streamlined interface for real-time data entry.
- **Team Managers and Coaches:** Monitoring team performance, tracking player impact, and maintaining seasonal records.

## Core Goals

- **Real-Time Accuracy:** Provide a robust and intuitive interface for tracking Goals, Behinds, and Total scores with automatic validation to eliminate errors.
- **Match Insights:** Visualize the flow of the game through interactive charts like score worms and detailed quarter-by-quarter breakdowns to help coaches understand momentum.
- **Data Persistence:** Enable comprehensive team management and historical record-keeping, allowing users to look back at previous seasons and matches.

## Key Features

- **Live Scoring Interface:** A dedicated screen for tracking scores as they happen, featuring large, easy-to-tap buttons for goals and behinds, with automatic score calculation.
- **Score Visualization:** An interactive "score worm" chart that plots the score over time, alongside statistical breakdowns for each quarter.
- **Team Management:** Tools to create and customize teams, including the ability to upload logos and apply team colors for a professional look.
- **Game History:** A searchable archive of all past games, with the ability to export or share results via social media or messaging apps.
- **Score Validation:** Built-in logic to ensure score entries are logical and consistent throughout the match duration.
